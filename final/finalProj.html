The ARMA 3DEN Editor

ARMA 3 contains a powerful mission editor that is almost equivalent to the developer toolkit used by Bohemia Interactive to make the game. It can be used to make anything from a training ground to test new content to making hours long missions complete with cutscenes and objectives. Everything in the game can be modified by the editor to whatever the user needs it to do. Beyond just changing the appearance of something or making it move to "X", the user can even set damage states, change AI behavior, and make them animate on queue. Several people have used this toolkit to make detailed mission packs that are almost as long and as complicated as the base game.
